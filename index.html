About Us

Teak Tock Furniture specializes in high-quality teak furniture. Our products are crafted with care and designed to last.

Reclaimed Teak is naturally seasoned, highly durable and among the most ecologically sustainable materials, that can be used to create elegant furniture. We resonate with the energy of teak, we revel in the uniqueness of each log. The grains on the surface of the wood, tell a story, and we are here to bring it to life! Our mission is to reclaim, recycle and recreate timeless handcrafted pieces that become heirlooms.
